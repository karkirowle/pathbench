### UASpeech Dataset Preparation

We processed the Universal Access Speech (UASpeech) dataset to construct standardized subsets for our analysis. Our processing focused on the isolated word production task, specifically utilizing recordings captured via the close-talking microphone (Channel M5) to match it with the original P-ESTOI paper. All transcripts were normalized by converting to lowercase and stripping punctuation to maintain strict consistency across control and pathological cohorts.

Alongside the audio, we integrated speaker-level metadata, mapping each pathological speaker to their corresponding age, gender, and standard objective intelligibility score.

**Note:** We are aware that there are noise-reduced and non-noise-reduced versions available, and we are currently unsure which one we used.
